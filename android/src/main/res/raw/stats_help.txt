<b>OPR</b> stands for Offensive Power Rating, which is a system to attempt to deduce the average point contribute of a team to an alliance.
<br/><br/>
<b>DPR</b> stands for Defensive Power Rating, which is a system to attempt to deduce the average defensive contribution of a team to an alliance.
<br/><br/>
<b>CCWM</b> stands for Calculated Contribution to Win Margin and is the difference between OPR and DPR.
<br/><br/>
These stats are an interesting way to slice the data for teams at particular events, but aren't a perfect system for assessing a team's performance or contribution.
